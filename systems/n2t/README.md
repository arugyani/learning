# NAND2Tetris

During college, I went through all the standard CS coursework of digital logic, computer architecture, operating systems, programming language paradigms, compilers, etc. 

As much as I would've liked to sink my teeth into each one, I was on a self-imposed timeline to graduate "on-time" and
made the mistake of taking lots of coursework each semester and having to put in a grind to make it through each class.

Looking back, I wish I could've spent more time sinking my teeth into a lot of these topics. The files available here represent my workthrough of Nand2Tetris as I aim to revisit old concepts and solidify some fundamentals.
